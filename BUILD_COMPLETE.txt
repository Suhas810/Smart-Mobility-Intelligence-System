📊 SAFETY SCORE FORMULA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Score = (10 - crime_rate) × 0.5  +  lighting × 0.3  +  (10 - traffic) × 0.2

Components:
  • Crime Rate (50%):  Inverse - lower crime = higher score
  • Lighting (30%):    Direct   - better lighting = higher score  
  • Traffic (20%):     Inverse  - less traffic = higher score

Risk Levels:
  Score > 7    →  🟢 SAFE     (Green)
  Score 4-7    →  🟡 MEDIUM   (Yellow)
  Score < 4    →  🔴 UNSAFE   (Red)
